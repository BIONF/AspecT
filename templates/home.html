AspecT and ClAssT


AspecT and ClAssT are both Tools to taxonomically assign Acinetobacter Input-Data using Bloom Filters. AspecT performs a taxonomic Assignment on the Acinetobacter Species-Level while ClAssT uses Strain-Typing to identify international Clones of A.baumannii.
